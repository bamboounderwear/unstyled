About Our Studio

Our Philosophy

Founded in 2020, our studio embraces the principles of Swiss Design, creating work that prioritizes clarity, precision, and objective presentation.

We believe that good design is as little design as possible. Our approach focuses on mathematical grid systems, typography, and the strategic use of white space to create meaningful visual communication.

Our Journey

2020

Studio founded with a commitment to Swiss Design principles and a focus on digital applications of grid systems.

2021

Expanded our team and client base, working with international brands to create cohesive visual systems.

2023

Launched our educational workshop series, teaching the principles of Swiss Design to the next generation of designers.

2025

Celebrating five years of design excellence with a retrospective exhibition and the publication of our first design manual.

Our Values

Clarity

We believe in clear communication through design, removing unnecessary elements to focus on what matters.

Precision

Every element is placed with purpose, using mathematical principles to create harmonious compositions.

Objectivity

Our designs present information in an objective manner, allowing content to speak for itself.

Innovation

While rooted in tradition, we constantly explore new applications of Swiss Design principles in contemporary contexts.
