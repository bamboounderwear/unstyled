Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

Our design process begins with research and understanding the problem space. We then develop a grid system that serves as the foundation for all design decisions. After establishing the typographic system, we iterate and refine until the solution meets all objectives.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A brand identity system typically takes 6-8 weeks, while a smaller digital project might be completed in 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, we work with clients around the world. Our systematic approach to design and clear communication processes make it easy to collaborate remotely while maintaining the highest quality standards.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we need to understand your project objectives, scope, timeline, and any specific requirements. The more detailed information you can provide, the more precise our estimate will be.
